**M05: Smart Cities and Transportation**

This module explores the integration of AI, IoT, and 5G connectivity to create more efficient, sustainable, and safer urban environments. 

**Core Learning Objectives**
* **Evolution of Urban Transit:** Analyzing the shift from manual, reactive traffic management to multi-modal, predictive, and AI-powered connected networks.
* **Smart City Building Blocks:** Understanding the infrastructure layer, including IoT sensor networks, Edge Computing, and Blockchain for data security.
* **AI in Transportation:** Utilizing Machine Learning for predictive traffic analytics and Computer Vision for pedestrian tracking and incident recognition.
* **Sustainability & Impact:** Evaluating how smart systems lead to a 20-30% reduction in travel time and a significant decrease in carbon emissions.

**Key Assignments & Research**

**1. Smart City Intelligence & Infrastructure (Class Notes)** 
A comprehensive summary of the building blocks of modern smart cities.

* **Focus:** Data management, Mobility as a Service (MaaS), and the role of digital twins in urban planning.
* **Technical Insights:** Exploration of V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle) and V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure) communications for collision avoidance.

**2. Comparative Analysis: Finance vs. Smart Cities**
A detailed document evaluating how AI transforms two different but interconnected sectors.

* **Finance Recap:** Real-time risk processing and economic forecasting using predictive analysis.
* **Urban Integration:** How AI enhances public safety through smart signals and environmental monitoring.
* **Security & Privacy:** Analysis of the essential protocols—like encryption and data anonymization—needed to maintain public trust in "Connected Cities."
