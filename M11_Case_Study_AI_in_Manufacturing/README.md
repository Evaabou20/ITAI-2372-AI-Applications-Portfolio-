**M11: Case Study - AI in Manufacturing**

This module explores the shift toward **Industry 4.0**, analyzing how Artificial Intelligence is transforming traditional production lines into smart, data-driven ecosystems. 

**Case Study Overview: Advantages of AI in Manufacturing**

The research focuses on the transition from reactive and fixed-schedule maintenance to intelligent, predictive systems. 

* **Predictive Maintenance:** Analysis of how machine learning and sensor data anticipate equipment failures before they occur, reducing unexpected downtime and extending machinery lifespan.
* **Industry 4.0 Integration:** Understanding the role of interconnected systems, big data analytics, and automation in modernizing the industrial landscape.
* **Operational Efficiency:** How AI-driven decision-making optimizes production speed, quality control, and supply chain logistics.

**Key Technical Insights**
* **From Reactive to Proactive:** Traditional manufacturing often fixed machines *after* they broke. This case study explores how AI uses real-time data to intervene *before* a failure happens.
* **Waste Reduction System:** Proposal for using AI to minimize material waste during the production process, contributing to both cost-efficiency and sustainability.
* **Data-Driven Decision Making:** Moving away from human-only supervision to collaborative systems where AI provides actionable insights for complex industrial problems.

**Challenges & Future Outlook**
* **Implementation Barriers:** Addressing the high initial costs, the need for specialized technical skills, and the complexity of integrating AI with legacy hardware.
* **Competitive Advantage:** Evaluation of how early adopters of AI in manufacturing gain a significant lead in the global market through increased productivity and lower operational costs.
