Archive of ITAI 2372: 13 modules exploring AI from philosophy to AGI. Includes a Netflix midterm and case studies in Finance, Healthcare, Manufacturing, and Education. 📁 **Portfolio Roadmap**
This repository is organized into 13 modules, following the course curriculum:
* **01 Foundations of AI:** Philosophy, the Turing Test, and Human Identity.
* **02 Trends & Breakthroughs:** Analysis of LLaMA 3, Claude 3.5, and Global AI Regulations.
* **03 Healthcare & Agriculture:** Precision farming and AI-driven diagnostics (Moderna Case).
* **04 AI in Finance:** Enterprise security at JPMorgan Chase and Stock Sentiment Tracking.
* **Midterm Project:** Strategic analysis of the **Netflix Recommendation Engine.**
* **06 Smart Cities (Mod 05):** IoT sensor networks, V2X communication, and urban logistics.
* **07 Media & Entertainment (Mod 06):** Generative AI, creative automation, and deepfake ethics.
* **08 Blockchain & AI (Mod 07):** Secure data infrastructure and decentralized ledgers (DLT).
* **09 AI in Retail (Mod 08):** Hyper-personalization, smart stores, and supply chain automation.
* **10 Case Study: Ethics (Mod 09):** Facial Recognition in Law Enforcement and bias mitigation.
* **11 Case Study: Public Sector (Mod 10):** BlueDot AI and COVID-19 pandemic early warning systems.
* **12 Case Study: Manufacturing (Mod 11):** Predictive Maintenance (PdM) and Industry 4.0.
* **13 Case Study: Future of AGI (Mod 12):** Leveraging Artificial General Intelligence in global education.

🛠️ **Core Competencies**
* **Ethical Governance:** Identifying and mitigating algorithmic bias and privacy risks.
* **Predictive Analytics:** Understanding the transition from reactive to predictive industrial operations.
* **Strategic Research:** Evaluating the business value and ROI of AI implementation.
