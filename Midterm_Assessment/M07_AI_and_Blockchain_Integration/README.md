**M07: AI and Blockchain Integration**

This module explores the synergy between Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain technology, focusing on how decentralization can enhance AI transparency and security. 

**Core Learning Objectives**
* **Blockchain Architecture:** Understanding Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT), cryptographic links, and the shift from centralized to decentralized systems.
* **Smart Contracts:** Analyzing how Ethereum-based programmable contracts automate transactions without intermediaries.
* 
**Convergence of AI & Blockchain:**
* **Data Integrity:** Using blockchain to provide a secure, immutable audit trail for AI training data. 
* **Decentralized AI:** Exploring marketplaces where AI models and datasets can be traded securely.
* **Security:** How AI enhances blockchain through fraud detection and smart contract auditing.

**Key Insights & Analysis**
* **Beyond Cryptocurrency:** While often associated with Bitcoin, blockchain's real value in this course lies in **Healthcare** (secure patient records), **Supply Chain** (transparent tracking), and **Government** (voting integrity).
  
**Technical Components:** 
* **Nodes:** Peer-to-peer validation without a central authority.
* **Immutability:** Once data is recorded in the ledger, it cannot be tampered with, solving the "trust" issue in AI data sourcing.
* **Risks & Challenges:** Addressing the "dark side" of the tech, including crypto scams, market manipulation, and the environmental impact of high-energy consensus mechanisms.
