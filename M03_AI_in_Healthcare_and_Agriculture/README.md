**M03: AI in Healthcare and Agriculture**

This module explores how AI is revolutionizing two critical sectors: life sciences and global food production. 

**Healthcare: From Diagnostics to Drug Discovery**
* **Accelerated Drug Development:** A primary case study for this module is **Moderna**, which used AI to expedite the development of its mRNA COVID-19 vaccine by optimizing design and production processes.
* **Precision Medicine:** Using genomic analysis and AI to tailor treatments and predict drug responses.
* **Remote Monitoring & Wearables:** Analyzing real-time data from IoT devices to detect abnormalities early and support predictive interventions.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Utilizing AI for hospital administration to manage massive data sets, automate scheduling, and reduce paperwork.

**Agriculture: Precision Farming & Sustainability**
* **Precision Agriculture:** Implementing data-driven decisions to optimize crop production and resource application.
* **Autonomous Monitoring:** Using drones and hyperspectral imaging for early crop disease detection and soil health assessment.
* **Smart Resource Management:** AI-powered irrigation and "See & Spray" technology (e.g., Blue River) that can reduce herbicide use by up to 90%.
* **Climate Resilience:** Developing AI models for hyperlocal weather forecasting and the creation of climate-resilient crop varieties.

**Assignment Overview: Industry Case Studies**

The included assignment provides a deep dive into practical AI implementations discussed in class:
* **The "Integrated Healthcare" Challenge:** Analysis of the current silos in hospital data and the potential for a future AI-powered ecosystem to coordinate doctors, records, and prescriptions.
* **Agricultural Automation:** Exploring the role of autonomous harvesters and AI in livestock monitoring to increase yield and reduce labor costs.
