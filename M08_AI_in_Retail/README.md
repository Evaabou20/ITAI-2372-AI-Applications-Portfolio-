**M08: AI in Retail** 

This module examines the digital transformation of the retail sector, focusing on how AI enhances the customer journey, optimizes supply chains, and enables "frictionless" shopping experiences.

**Core Learning Objectives**
* **The Customer Journey:** Analyzing the shift from traditional brick-and-mortar shopping to AI-driven personalized experiences.
* **Predictive Analytics:** Understanding how retailers use historical data to forecast demand, reducing waste and ensuring product availability.
* **Computer Vision & Sensors:** Exploring autonomous checkout systems (like Amazon Go) that allow for "grab-and-go" shopping without traditional cashiers.
* **Virtual Try-Ons:** The use of AR and AI to allow customers to "test" products (makeup, clothing, glasses) digitally, significantly reducing return rates. 

**Key Insights & Analysis**
* **Inventory Management:** AI-powered systems can predict stockouts before they happen and optimize delivery routes, making the global supply chain more resilient.
* **Hyper-Personalization:** Beyond simple recommendations, AI analyzes real-time behavior to offer targeted discounts and loyalty rewards that increase customer lifetime value.
* **The Future of Work:** Discussing the transition of retail roles from manual checkout and stocking to technical oversight and customer experience management.

**Case Study: Amazon Go**
* **Tech Stack:** Integration of deep learning algorithms, sensor fusion, and computer vision.
* **Impact:** Eliminating "waiting in line" as a barrier to purchase, while providing the retailer with granular data on how customers move through a physical space.
